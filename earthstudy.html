Exploring Earth's oceans in the world.

What is a Ocean?

An ocean is a large body of saltwater, covering nearly 71% of the Earth's surface and containing about 97% of its water.

Scientific Deep Dive

  • Geology: ocean floor is shaped by plate tectonics, featuring diverse landscapes like mid-ocean ridges, trenches, and abyssal plains
  • Ecology: The deep ocean is characterized by high pressure, low temperatures (around 2-4°C), and the lack of sunlight.
  • Climate: The ocean absorbs a vast amount of solar radiation, acting as the largest solar energy collector on Earth. It absorbs approximately 91% of the excess heat in the Earth's climate system.

🌊 Indian Ocean
