About Us

Welcome to ASTRO V – The Atlas Curators, a creative space where imagination meets exploration. We are a team of passionate learners who believe in reimagining how people experience Earth — through the vastness of space. In our project, each planet of the solar system is uniquely paired with real-world environments like deserts, glaciers, islands, and forests. For example, Mercury reflects the extreme landscapes of deserts, while Neptune represents the serene beauty of islands. This project is more than just visual — it's an educational journey across continents and cultures, wrapped in a cosmic theme. Each planet profile showcases hidden gems from the seven continents, enriched with fun facts, and captivating imagery. We aim to spark curiosity, inspire travel, and promote learning in a fun and interactive way. As students building this remotely, we combined our individual strengths to design a unique atlas — not just of Earth, but of imagination, science, and storytelling. Join us as we map the world... one planet at a time.
